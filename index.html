ORU ACM-W

Our ORU ACM-W Chapter is designed to be a group of students who want to learn computer skills and build a community. ACM-W aims to provide opportunities for skill development and professional growth, and foster a supportive community for women interested in technology. We provide support to our members and connect you with like-minded peers and professionals.
